# **Medical & Clinical Repositories**

Access to medical and clinical data is often restricted due to privacy regulations. However, several open datasets, controlled-access repositories, and synthetic data sources exist for research purposes.

---

## **Medical Imaging Repositories**

| Repository | Description | Access | Link |
|------------|-------------|--------|------|
| **The Cancer Imaging Archive (TCIA)** | Large archive of medical images for cancer research (CT, MRI, PET) | Open / Controlled | [cancerimagingarchive.net](https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/) |
| **UK Biobank Imaging** | Brain, cardiac, and body MRI from 100,000+ participants | Controlled | [ukbiobank.ac.uk](https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/) |
| **OpenNeuro** | Open platform for sharing neuroimaging data (MRI, EEG, MEG) in BIDS format | Open | [openneuro.org](https://openneuro.org/) |
| **Grand Challenge** | Platform hosting medical imaging challenges with curated datasets | Varies | [grand-challenge.org](https://grand-challenge.org/) |
| **TCGA (The Cancer Genome Atlas)** | Multi-omics cancer data including histopathology images | Controlled | [cancer.gov/tcga](https://www.cancer.gov/ccg/research/genome-sequencing/tcga) |

|------------|-------------|--------|------|
| **MIMIC-IV** | Critical care database with de-identified EHR data from Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center | Controlled (credentialing) | [physionet.org/content/mimiciv](https://physionet.org/content/mimiciv/) |
| **eICU Collaborative Research Database** | Multi-center ICU dataset with physiological and clinical data | Controlled | [physionet.org/content/eicu-crd](https://physionet.org/content/eicu-crd/) |

| **ClinicalTrials.gov** | Registry of clinical studies with protocols and summary results | Open | [clinicaltrials.gov](https://clinicaltrials.gov/) |
| **EU Clinical Trials Register** | European register of clinical trials with protocols | Open | [clinicaltrialsregister.eu](https://www.clinicaltrialsregister.eu/) |

## **Important Note on Data Access**

Many medical datasets require:
1. **Institutional ethics approval** or IRB review
2. **Data use agreements (DUA)** specifying permitted uses
3. **CITI training** or equivalent research ethics certification
4. **Controlled-access applications** with justification of research need

Always check access requirements before planning analyses with clinical data.
