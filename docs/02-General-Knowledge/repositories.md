title: Repository collections
---

| Resource | Description | Link |
|---|---|---|
| Registry of Research Data Repositories (re3data) | Global registry of research data repositories across various subject-specific disciplines. For researchers, this resource is a starting point for finding repositories that may contain relevant searchable data and for depositing their research data. | https://www.re3data.org/ |
| FAIRsharing | Manually curated searchable registry for data and metadata standards, linked to repositories and data policies. It is focused on life sciences, environmental sciences, biosciences and medical sciences.| https://fairsharing.org/ |
| DataCite Repository Finder | DataCite Commons indexes datasets registered with a DOI with DataCite, CrossRef, or one of six other scholarly DOI agencies. DataCite links the repository to information in re3data. | https://repositoryfinder.datacite.org/ |
